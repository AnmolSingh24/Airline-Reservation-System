Conditions & Carriages

General Terms

1. By using Blue Wing Airways' services, passengers agree to comply with the terms and conditions
outlined in our General Terms of Service, which can be found on our website or provided upon request.

2. Blue Wing Airways reserves the right to make changes to its services, schedules, fares, and policies.
It is the passengers' responsibility to review and stay informed about any updates or modifications to the general terms.

3. Passengers are required to follow all applicable laws, regulations, and security measures during their
travel with Blue Wing Airways, and any violation may result in denial of boarding or legal action as deemed necessary.

Baggage Policy

1. Checked Baggage

Blue Wing Airways allows passengers to check in a certain number of bags free of charge,
depending on the ticket class. Please refer to our website or contact our customer service for specific details.

Each checked bag must adhere to the maximum weight and size limits set by Blue Wing Airways.
Exceeding these limits may result in additional fees or restrictions.

It is recommended to label your checked bags with your name and contact information to facilitate
identification and contact in case of any mishandling or loss.

2. Lost or Damaged Baggage

1. In the unfortunate event of lost or damaged baggage, please report the issue to our customer service immediately.
We will initiate a thorough investigation to locate your lost bag or assist with the process of filing a claim for damaged items.

2. Blue Wing Airways is committed to resolving baggage mishandling incidents promptly. We will make every effort to
reunite passengers with their lost bags as quickly as possible and provide compensation for any documented damages,
in accordance with our policy.

3. To ensure a smooth claims process, please retain your baggage claim tags and provide any supporting documentation,
such as purchase receipts or photographs of damaged items, when filing a claim for lost or damaged baggage.
Our customer service team will guide you through the necessary steps.

3. Prohibited Items

If you have any questions regarding prohibited items, please contact our customer service.

Check-In and Boarding

Check-In Policy

Passengers are required to check in at the airport at least 2 hours prior
to the scheduled departure time. This allows sufficient time for security
checks and baggage handling.

Late check-ins may result in the cancellation of your reservation.

Boarding Policy

Boarding begins 30 minutes before the scheduled departure time.

Passengers must be present at the boarding gate and ready for
boarding at least 20 minutes before the scheduled departure time.
Failure to do so may result in missing the flight.

Flight Cancellations and Delays

1. In the event of flight cancellations or significant delays, Blue Wing Airways will make every effort to
notify affected passengers in a timely manner via the contact information provided during booking.
It is recommended to keep your contact information up to date to ensure effective communication.

2. Blue Wing Airways understands the inconvenience caused by flight cancellations and delay and will
strive to provide alternative arrangements, such as rebooking on the next available flight or offering
compensation in accordance with applicable regulations and our policy.

3. Please note that flight cancellations or delays due to unforeseen circumstances, including weather
conditions, air traffic control, or safety concerns, may be beyond Blue Wing Airways' control.
We apologize for any inconvenience caused and assure you that the safety and well-being of our passengers are our top priorities.
