GRIEVANCE RESOLUTION

Grievance Resolution Process

At Air India, our goal is to resolve customer concerns in a timely manner. Our customer service team is supported by
authorities dedicated to helping resolve grievances. Here are the steps to submit a complaint—and escalate it if necessary:

Submit your grievance our Customer Service Portal
Go to Blue Wing's Customer Service Portal to share your grievance. When you do, you'll receive a unique Case ID number. We ask for your
email address so the customer service agent reviewing your grievance can follow-up or share issue resolution via email within 24 hours.

For comments concerning Civil Aviation Requirement, Section 3—Air Transport, Series M Part IV, please contact:

Mumbai Officer
email: mumbaiofficer@bluewing.com

Appellate Authority
email: appellateauthority@bluewing.com
