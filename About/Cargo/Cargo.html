CARGO OPERATION

Cargo Operations

Blue Wing cargo is one of the world's leading cargo carriers with an extensive global as well as domestic network. We possess
experienced & skilled employees to serve growing list of customers as we offer a high-quality of air freight services with premium
facilities.

Blue Wing is an active member of IATA as well as ICH. Air India carries majorly all types of cargo & follows IATA Regulations for acceptance
of cargo besides other standards.

Blue Wing has a dedicated modern fleet of Boeing and Airbus aircrafts with huge cargo capacities available to carry loads to more than
39 online International as well as 38 domestic destinations currently besides hundreds of offline trucking points as we have tied up
with various trucking companies worldwide to carry Cargo safely.

Blue Wing to be digitally up to date with Industry practice currently uses the state-of-the-art technology for cargo activities called
Logistic Management System (LMS) for cargo sales & operational purpose available 24X7 to serve our customers better. The LMS
system supports EDI interface with Indian customs. The LMS is in use, providing an end-to-end solution with accuracy and availability
of real-time information with reference to booking, acceptance, stowage, unitization, handling, manifestation, carriage, transhipment,
delivery etc., within Air India network..
